Color Changer

Improve your scanning by reacting to the color when receiving the ball in-between touches.

  • Red: An opposition player is close behind and you should pass the ball back.
  • Green: There's open space behind and you can turn and linger a couple of seconds the ball.
  • Yellow: An opposition player approaching and you can either pass back or perform a feint to evade the player.
  • Blue: A teammate is asking for the ball from the sides.
